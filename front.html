Mandatory Eligibility criteria for Promotion / Increments

  1. 2 Research paper publications in Scopus or Web of Science indexed journal during the assessment period
  2. At least ONE FDP per semester (minimum 5 days duration) conducted by IITs / NITs and premium institutions (NIRF Ranked Institutions 1-100).
  3. Faculty members should ensure a minimum of 50% marks in at least 03 assessment heads.
